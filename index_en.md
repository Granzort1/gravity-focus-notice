# Update Notices

## 2026-04-22 Update Notice

Hello, I'm the app developer.

In this update, the Plan menu has been completely redesigned as a daily time-block schedule.

## Update Details

1) Plan menu redesigned — Daily time schedule
- The old Plan / Do menus weren't being used much, so I rebuilt the Plan menu around a more practical purpose: laying out your day as a time-block schedule.
- As shown in the example below, you split the day into time slots and write down what you'll do in each slot to design the whole day.

### Daily schedule example

| Time | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| 5–6 AM | Sleep |
| 6–7 AM | 6:30 wake up, shower, get dressed |
| 7–8 AM | 7:00–7:30 breakfast / 7:30 walk the dog / leave home by 7:50 |
| 8–9 AM | Train to work, arrive at the office |
| 9–10 AM | 9:00–9:10 plan the day / 9:10–10:00 emails & calls |
| 10–11 AM | 10:00–10:30 meeting |
| 11 AM–12 PM | 11:55–12:00 add tasks / engine oil change |
| 12–1 PM | Lunch, break, short walk |
| 1–2 PM | 1:00–1:45 work on monthly report summary / 1:45–2:00 emails & calls |
| 2–3 PM | Work tasks as needed |
| 3–4 PM | 3:00–3:30 meeting / 3:30–4:00 write up meeting notes, update schedule |
| 4–5 PM | 4:45–5:00 emails & calls, check tomorrow's schedule |
| 5–6 PM | Train home |
| 6–7 PM | 6:00 pick up clothes from the laundry, get home, walk the dog, check mail |
| 7–8 PM | 7:00–7:30 dinner / 7:30–8:00 house cleaning, downtime |
| 8–9 PM | 8:00–8:30 downtime (8:25 change into workout clothes) / 8:30–9:00 treadmill |
| 9–10 PM | Shower, pick tomorrow's outfit, leisure |
| 10–11 PM | Leisure, in bed by 11 |

- Laying out your day this way makes it obvious what to do and when, so it's harder to postpone or lose track of things.
- You don't have to follow this example exactly — just fill in times and tasks that fit your own routine.

## We'd love to hear from you

If there's anything inconvenient about the new Plan menu, or if you have ideas for improvement, please let us know. You can use the mail icon at the top of the Settings menu or the "Send Feedback" option at the bottom. Every piece of feedback helps make the app better.

Thank you.
